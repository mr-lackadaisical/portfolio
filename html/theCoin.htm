THE COIN

My grandpa had bipolar disorder. My dad told me that like a coin grandpa had two sides , whichever side I saw it was the same coin, the same person, my grandpa . He passed away when i was 8 years old. During the lockdown dad was telling us about his childhood and grandpa and I wanted to make his portrait. I wanted to show both sides of my grandpa in such a way that each side can be distinctly visible, so if you place a mirror in the centre you can see both sides, the heads and tails.

Heads

In this stage he used to be full of energy and could hardly sleep. He used to talk a lot and used to tinker with things. He used to be annoyed all the time and got very angry and upset very frequently.
